What is BEAM Proxy?

BEAM Proxy is a private, authenticated forward proxy hosted on a DigitalOcean VPS. It routes your web traffic through a dedicated server, giving you a consistent cloud IP without the complexity of a full VPN.

Why BEAM?

Private by Design

Access is protected by authentication. This is not an open or shared proxy — only authorized users can connect.

Simple & Lightweight

No client software, no tunneling overhead. Configure it once in your browser or application and you’re ready to go.

Consistent Cloud IP

Route traffic through a single DigitalOcean IP for testing, access control, and controlled outbound connections.

Transparent & Honest

BEAM Proxy does not promise anonymity or bypassing protections. It provides predictable infrastructure — nothing more, nothing less.

How It Works

1. Authenticate

Connect using your assigned username and password.

2. Route Traffic

Your web requests are forwarded through the BEAM Proxy server.

3. Reach the Internet

Websites see the proxy’s IP — not your local device.
