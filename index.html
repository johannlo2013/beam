Why BEAM?

Private by Design

Access is protected by authentication. This is not an open or shared proxy — only students can connect.

Simple & Lightweight

No client software, no tunneling overhead. Configure it once in your system settings and you’re ready to go.

Bypass Internet Blocks

Routes traffic through external servers, allowing you to play blocked gaming sites and apps, like Roblox.

Policy Risks

BEAM Proxy does not promise anonymity or bypassing protections. It provides predictable infrastructure — nothing more, nothing less. Do not use this on campus, only to provide a quick solution to wrongly blocked sites.

How It Works

1. Authenticate

Connect using your assigned username and password.

2. Route Traffic

Your web requests are forwarded through the BEAM Proxy server.

3. Reach the Internet

Websites see the proxy’s IP — not your local device.
